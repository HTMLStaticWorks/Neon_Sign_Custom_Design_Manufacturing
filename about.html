The Art of Manufacturing

Founded on the intersection of traditional artistry and modern LED technology, Neon Studio began with a simple mission: to make premium custom lighting accessible to businesses and individuals alike.

Every sign that leaves our facility is meticulously handcrafted by experienced artisans. We utilize proprietary LED flex technology that provides superior brightness, durability, and energy efficiency compared to traditional glass neon.

Sustainability

Unlike traditional glass neon that relies on toxic gasses, our advanced LED silicone flex is 100% eco-friendly, fully recyclable, and consumes 80% less energy.

Precision

Computer-aided design meets handcrafted artisan assembly. Every aerospace-grade acrylic backboard is CNC-routed to the exact millimeter for flawless integration.

Longevity

We do not manufacture temporary event props. We build permanent architectural lighting solutions rigorously rated for over 50,000 hours of continuous, vibrant glow.
