Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing, shipping, and installation.

Our pricing includes the fully assembled custom LED neon sign, a transparent acrylic backing cut to shape, a standardized power supply with appropriate regional plugs, and a basic wall mounting kit. Medium and Large sizes also include remote dimmers for free.

We offer standard free shipping globally on all Medium and Large signs. Small signs have a flat $30 shipping fee. For expedited or priority commercial shipping, rates are calculated at checkout based on location.
